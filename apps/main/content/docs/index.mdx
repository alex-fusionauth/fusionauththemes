This guide is to assist those maintaining FusionAuth Themes.

## What is FusionAuth

FusionAuth is a Customer Identity and Access Management (CIAM) with hybrid, single-tenant deployment you can dev and test anywhere.

### FusionAuth Custom Themes

FusionAuth provides the ability to fully customize the look and feel of your FusionAuth login experience.
You can find guides on how to customize the look and feel of your FusionAuth login experience in the [FusionAuth Themes Overview](https://fusionauth.io/docs/customize/look-and-feel/) section of the docs.

### FusionAuth Themes (this site)

This site was built to showcase how to customize the look and feel of your FusionAuth login experience.
We have provided a few examples to get you started. This documentation will walk you through how to setup a
FusionAuth instance locally, run scripts to setup a custom theme, and deploy it to your production environment.
